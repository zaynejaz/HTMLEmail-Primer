A Note from the Founders

Hi Friend —

Thanks for joining us on the Primer journey.

We'll have more to share soon, but for now, we want you to know that we started Primer because we believe something quite simple: kids are remarkable — and our current education system is underestimating them.

We're building a new kind of education platform for kids — starting with homeschoolers.

In the coming months, we'll be sharing more about our vision, our product, and some inspiring stories of folks who have taken non-traditional paths in their education.

In the meantime, we've got two resources for you: Primer Library and Primer Navigator, which you've probably already seen. If you think they could be helpful to your friends and family, we'd love for you to spread the word.

Finally, we'd love to hear from you — just reply to this email, or say hi on Twitter.


Ryan & Maksim
Co-Founders, Primer


P.S. We're hiring. We're looking for ambitious folks who think differently, no education experience necessary.
